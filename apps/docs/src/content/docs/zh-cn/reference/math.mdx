## 对齐

$$
\begin{aligned}
H(X)
  &= -\sum_{x} p(x)\log p(x) \\
  &= \mathbb{E}_{x\sim p}\bigl[-\log p(x)\bigr]
\end{aligned}
$$

\begin{cases}
\texttt{allow}  & \text{if } \mathrm{risk}(a) \le 1 \\
\texttt{approve}& \text{if } 2 \le \mathrm{risk}(a) \le 3 \\
\texttt{deny}   & \text{if } \mathrm{risk}(a) \ge 4
\end{cases}
$$
